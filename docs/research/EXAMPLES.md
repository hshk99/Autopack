# Research Examples

This document provides an overview of the example research sessions available in the repository. These examples are designed to illustrate the application of research methodologies in different contexts.

## Available Examples

1. [Market Research Example](../../examples/market_research_example.md)
   - Demonstrates how to conduct market research to identify potential opportunities and threats in the industry.

2. [App Research Example](../../examples/app_research_example.md)
   - Provides a detailed walkthrough of researching user needs and app functionalities to enhance user experience.

3. [Autopack Research Example](../../examples/autopack_research_example.md)
   - Explores the research process behind the development of the Autopack framework, focusing on automation and AI integration.

Each example includes a narrative that guides you through the research process, highlighting key steps and considerations.

Refer to the individual example files for more detailed information.
