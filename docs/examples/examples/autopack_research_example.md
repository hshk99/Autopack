# Autopack Research Example

## Overview

This example outlines the research process behind the development of the Autopack framework. The focus is on integrating automation and AI to streamline software development.

## Research Process

1. **Identify Automation Opportunities**
   - Analyze current software development workflows to identify repetitive tasks.
   - Evaluate the potential for automation to improve efficiency.

2. **Explore AI Integration**
   - Research AI models that can assist in code generation and review.
   - Consider the feasibility of integrating AI into existing workflows.

3. **Prototype Development**
   - Develop a prototype to test the integration of AI and automation.
   - Gather feedback from initial users to refine the approach.

4. **Iterate and Improve**
   - Use feedback to make iterative improvements to the framework.
   - Focus on enhancing usability and expanding functionality.

5. **Document Findings**
   - Compile research findings and document the development process for future reference.
