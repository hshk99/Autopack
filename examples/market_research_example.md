# Market Research Example

## Purpose

This example provides a framework for conducting market research to identify opportunities and threats within an industry.

## Steps for Conducting Market Research

1. **Define Research Goals**
   - Determine what you want to learn from the market research.
   - Set clear objectives to guide the research process.

2. **Identify Target Market**
   - Define the demographics and characteristics of your target market.
   - Use surveys and focus groups to gather data on consumer preferences.

3. **Analyze Industry Trends**
   - Research current trends and future predictions in the industry.
   - Identify potential opportunities and threats based on these trends.

4. **Evaluate Competitors**
   - Conduct a competitive analysis to understand the strengths and weaknesses of key players in the market.
   - Identify gaps in the market that your product or service could fill.

5. **Compile and Present Findings**
   - Organize the research data into a comprehensive report.
   - Present findings to stakeholders to inform strategic decision-making.
