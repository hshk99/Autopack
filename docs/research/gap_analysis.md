# Gap Analysis Documentation

## Overview

Gap analysis is a critical component of the research process, aimed at identifying missing information or categories in the collected data. This ensures comprehensive coverage and highlights areas needing further investigation.

## Features

- **Identification of Missing Categories**: Detects absent types or categories in the dataset.
- **Data Completeness Check**: Evaluates the completeness of the collected data against predefined criteria.

## Methodology

The gap analysis process involves comparing the types and categories of collected data against a predefined list of expected categories. Any discrepancies are flagged for further investigation.

## Importance

Conducting a thorough gap analysis helps in ensuring that the research is exhaustive and that no critical areas are overlooked.
